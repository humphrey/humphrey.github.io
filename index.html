BlitzScorer.com

An unofficial scoring app for the Dutch Blitz card game, but works great for most other games too! Such as Hearts and 500. A friend also uses it to keep score while on the golf course!

  • Up to 8 players
  • Record scores for each round. Totals shown at bottom
  • Simple +1, +5, +10, and -1, -5, -10 buttons for adjusting score
  • Change a players name by tapping the name.

  • Client: Myself
  • Date: 2015-09-01
  • Category: Web Development
